Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 9, 2024

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Company name (optional)
  • Full name (optional)

1.2 Email Content

When you scan emails, we temporarily process:

  • Email text content
  • Email headers (for .eml files)
  • URLs contained in emails

Data Retention: Scan results are retained for 30 days, then automatically deleted. Email content is encrypted at rest.

1.3 Usage Data

We collect:

  • Scan history and results
  • API usage statistics
  • IP addresses (for security)
  • Browser/device information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide phishing detection services
  • Improve our machine learning models
  • Prevent abuse and ensure security
  • Send service notifications (not marketing)
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • AES-256 encryption for stored email content
  • HTTPS/TLS for data in transit
  • Bcrypt password hashing
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and audit logging

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your data.

We may share data only:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal requirements
  • With service providers (under strict NDAs)
  • In anonymized/aggregated form for research

5. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Delete your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data
  • Objection: Opt-out of data processing

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@shadowops.security

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies for:

  • Authentication (session management)
  • Security (CSRF protection)
  • Preferences (UI settings)

No third-party tracking cookies are used.

7. Data Retention

  • Scan Results: 30 days (configurable)
  • Account Data: Until account deletion
  • Audit Logs: 90 days (for security)

8. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States. We ensure adequate protections through standard contractual clauses.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be notified via email.

11. Contact Us

For privacy questions:

  • Email: privacy@shadowops.security
  • Data Protection Officer: dpo@shadowops.security
